About

Greetings! I’m Shankul Patel, an aspiring AI researcher, currently pursuing Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) at Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) department of IIT Roorkee. Completed Master of Technology (Data Science) from Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi in 2025. Also did Master of Computer Application (MCA) from JNU in 2023, where I built a solid foundation in computer science and computational thinking.

My research interests include Deep Learning, Multimodal Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), Computer Vision, and Embedded Systems, with a particular focus on their applications in real-world challenges. I am passionate about exploring the intersection of these domains and leveraging cutting-edge techniques to solve complex problems.
