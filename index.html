Vishwavidyalaya Anusandhan Utsav 2023 @Dr Ambedkar International Center New Delhi (Apr 24, 2023)

Participated in the Utsav 2023 by DST (Department of Science and Technology) at Dr. Ambedkar International Center New Delhi. Represented the School of Computer and Systems Sciences (SC&SS) JNU New Delhi by presenting the poster at the JNU stall from the Microsystems Lab under the supervision of Dr. Poonam Agarwal (currently Associate Professor at the SC&SS, JNU). Hon'ble Minister Dr. Jitendra Singh launched the SUPREME program.

RIISE 2023 Program @IIIT Delhi (May 11, 2023)

Prof. Manindra Agrawal (IITK) was the keynote speaker highlighting the effectiveness and relevance of 'Research from First Principles' in the era of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. He shared the thoughts on how he predicted the waves of CORONA epidemic just by using simple differential equations. It was a very refressing session to hear from him. In the Industry 4.0 session, speakers articulated how AI is being used in 4.0. There were many poster presentations on many topics including AI, Communications etc.
